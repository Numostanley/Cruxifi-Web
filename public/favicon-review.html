Design decision

The mark is a geometric stroke arc, not a letterform from the Outfit typeface. The risk: at a glance it could read as an unfinished circle rather than a C. The justification: at 16 px, typographic letterforms lose fine optical details; a pure stroke arc stays crisp and distinct at every size. It also echoes the glowing ring motif already in the hero section — the same visual language, scaled down to a mark. Infrastructure brands need marks that double as loading indicators, watermarks, and monograms. A stroke arc does all three.
